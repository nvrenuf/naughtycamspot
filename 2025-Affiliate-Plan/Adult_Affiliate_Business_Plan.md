## Phase 2: Scaling Content and Paid Traffic (3–6 Months)

### Objectives
- Expand content libraries.
- Begin small-scale paid advertising campaigns.
- Reinvest affiliate revenue to grow traffic.

### Steps

1. **Expand Blogs**

2. **Run Paid Ads**
   - Platforms: **TrafficJunky** and **ExoClick**.

3. **Optimize for SEO**
   - Use tools like **RankMath** or **Yoast SEO** for on-page optimization.
   - Target underserved niches with long-tail keywords.

4. **Expand Affiliate Networks**
   - Sign up for additional programs:
     - **FetishHits**: High-paying niche programs.
