# Model Revenue Architecture — Pinned Note

This note captures the current strategic direction for NaughtyCamSpot so it can be revisited and expanded later.

## Positioning shift

Do not frame NCS as a basic promotion service.

Preferred framing:
- growth system
- revenue system
- model revenue architecture
- fan revenue system
- revenue operating system for performers
- paid fan world
- relationship infrastructure
- better model business
- smarter creator system
- premium growth line
- new revenue line
- fan retention and revenue system

Core message:

**We are not building a promo service. We are building a model revenue system that helps creators earn through discovery, live conversion, VIP retention, personalized offers, recurring events, and passive-income layers.**

## Business model shift

Old model:
- go live
- hope people show up
- hope they tip
- repeat tomorrow

Better model:
- get discovered in smarter ways
- convert viewers better
- move the right fans into deeper paid layers
- keep them coming back
- give them multiple ways to spend
- make money even when the model is offline

The live room is the front door / engine, not the whole business.
