## Personal safety baseline

- Keep legal name and creator identity separated.
- Use unique passwords and 2FA on every platform.
- Define your hard boundaries in writing before launch.

## Well-being matters for consistency

Create downtime blocks and recovery windows in your calendar. Sustainable pacing beats short burnout spikes.
